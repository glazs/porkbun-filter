# Privacy Policy for Porkbun Price Filter

**Effective Date: January 26, 2026**

Porkbun Price Filter (the "Extension") is designed with a focus on privacy.

### 1. Information Collection and Use
We do **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or information about your computer. 
- All filtering logic happens locally on your machine within the browser's context.
- No data is sent to any external servers.

### 2. Permissions
The extension requires the following permissions to function:
- `scripting`: Required to inject the filtering logic into the Porkbun search results page.
- `activeTab`: Required to interact only with the tab you are currently viewing when you click the extension.
- `host_permissions` for `porkbun.com`: Required to allow the extension to run its filtering logic on the Porkbun website.

### 3. Third-Party Services
The Extension does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

### 4. Disclaimer of Warranty
The Extension is provided "as is", without warranty of any kind, express or implied. The developer shall not be liable for any results, including but not limited to, incorrect filtering or functional issues on the website. Use at your own risk.
